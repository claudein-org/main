claudein.org – Privacy Policy
Last updated: 2026-06-17

WHAT WE COLLECT
We collect only what is necessary to provide the service:
  - Your LinkedIn access token (stored in a session cookie, never persisted to disk)
  - Your LinkedIn profile ID and display name (used to post on your behalf)

We do not collect email addresses, passwords, or any other personal data.

HOW WE USE IT
  - To authenticate you with LinkedIn via OAuth 2.0
  - To post content to LinkedIn on your behalf when you run the CLI

WHO WE SHARE IT WITH
Nobody. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.

DATA RETENTION
Session tokens are stored in a short-lived cookie that expires when your browser
session ends. We retain no data on our servers after you log out.
